SMS Deal Notifications Policy

Consent

By providing your phone number, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages about deals, discounts, and promotional offers.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on available deals and promotions. You may receive up to several messages per week.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation message.

Help

For help or questions, reply HELP to any message.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us through the same channel you used to sign up.
